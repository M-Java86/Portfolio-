About

Hello, and welcome! Thanks for stopping by. Let's start with a little about my background and transition to software development.

2017 I gained a huge interest in coding, I began teaching myself by watching youtube vidoes, purchasing books such as Coding for beginners, JavaScript for beginners and HTML for beginners. I also went to local meetups in the Atlanta, GA metro area. I'm excited about building websites, graphic design and apps while working with a team on interesting projects..

I hope to make the world a better place through the use of technology. Here's to learning, new experiences, and all the challenges that lie ahead!
